Bio

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Imperial Computer Vision and Learning Lab of Imperial College London working with Dr. Tae-Kyun Kim. Previously, I was a Postdoctoral researcher at Department of Automation of Tsinghua University. I obtained my Ph.D. from Tsinghua University under the supervision of Prof. Jie Zhou and Prof. Jiwen Lu. My research interest lies in 3D vision on human - the perception of 3D humans by machine and large scale retrieval.
